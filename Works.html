Alnakeel Resort

A boutique resort in the middle of the largest palm oasis in the world, we design it to provide a unique and luxurious experience for guests. The resort is designed to blend in with the natural surroundings and offer guests a peaceful and relaxing environment. The resort features private villas that are surrounded by lush greenery and palm trees. The villas are designed with modern amenities such as private pools, outdoor showers, and spacious living areas. The resort also features a spa that offers guests a range of treatments to help them relax and rejuvenate. Additionally, the resort can offer guests a range of activities such as hiking, harvesting dates, and many more to explore the surrounding oasis.
