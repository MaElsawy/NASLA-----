Overview

About Nasla

Born from the historic heart of Al-Ahsa, NASLA was founded with the ambition to become a leader in Saudi Arabia's tourism development and experiential travel. Acquired by Salem Balhamer Holding Group and headquartered in Dammam, NASLA aligns perfectly with the group’s vision of diversifying investments and supporting Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030.

Innovation, sustainability, and cultural authenticity are at the core of NASLA. We don’t just create destinations – we craft experiences.

Our Message

Our mission is to transform the way people perceive and engage with travel and tourism. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, sustainable practices, and a deep understanding of local cultures, we aspire to craft immersive and unforgettable experiences that resonate with visitors on a profound level.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in shaping the future of tourism in Saudi Arabia and beyond, while we envision our projects seamlessly integrating with its natural and cultural surroundings.

Our Values

Core values are the set of ideals, principles, and beliefs that form the broad outlines that direct and control our behavior, guide decisions, and provide a reference for resolution and weighting when there is disagreement about a specific decision. Values stem from our Islamic and Arab beliefs and culture, our national identity, and the cumulative legacy practiced as behavior since its founding by the founder of the group and the Chairman of its Board of Directors, Sheikh Salem Balhamer, may God bless him and the team that started with him. We strive and work hard to ensure its sustainability across generations. Our core value system in the Salem Balhamer Group has come to include:

Commitment Transparency Credibility Sustainability Innovation Respect Professionalism

CEO MESSAGE

Guiding Our Journey: A Message from the CEO

Our CEO Message

“Driven by Vision 2030, NASLA aspires to contribute to transform Saudi Arabia into a world-class tourist destination. While The Kingdom's ambitious plan aims to attract 150 million visitors by 2030, a significant increase from the current 100 million.”

We at NASLA are committed to developing tourism experiences that celebrate the rich cultural heritage and traditions of Saudi Arabia, while remaining sensitive to the diverse backgrounds of our guests. By fostering a deep understanding of both Arabic and international cultures, we strive to create a welcoming and enriching environment for all.

Through continuous improvement in our people, processes, and technology, NASLA is dedicated to achieving excellence in the tourism sector. We actively seek partnerships with local and international organizations to share best practices and achieve our shared goals.

Ali Alsaileek
